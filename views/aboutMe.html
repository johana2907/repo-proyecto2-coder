About Me

Who Am I ?

I am I. JOHANA MIDEROS S., a sports coach, hydrotherapy instructor, professional dancer, and pole fitness teacher. With over 20 years of experience in the world of movement, I am passionate about helping people achieve their physical goals, improve their health, and enjoy dance and exercise as a form of personal expression. My approach is based on discipline, creativity, and overall well-being.

Values

commitment

As a coach and dance instructor, my commitment is to motivate my students to reach their best version, combining strength, technique, and creativity. I work with dedication to foster their overall well-being and a love for movement, guiding them in a personalized and passionate way toward their goals.

Quality

My quality as a sports coach and dance instructor is based on my experience, passion, and personalized approach, providing each student with training tailored to their needs and goals, always with professionalism and dedication.

Excellence

My excellence as a sports coach and dance instructor is reflected in my constant commitment to technique, progress, and the well-being of my students, always guiding them toward success with passion and professionalism.
